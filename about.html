About BelPulse

Your window into the Belgian macroeconomic pulse

BelPulse is an independent intelligence platform dedicated to providing real-time transparency into Belgium's economic and financial health. Our mission is to democratize access to complex macroeconomic data through intuitive visualizations and expert commentary.

We aggregate data from the National Bank of Belgium (NBB), Statbel, the Federal Planning Bureau, and international institutions to offer a comprehensive view of the national landscape.

  • 📊 Real-time data synchronization
  • 🔍 Deep-dive structural analysis
  • 🌍 Multi-institution forecast consensus
